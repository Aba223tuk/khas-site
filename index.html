Our Story

A TASTE OF KHORASAAN

طعم خوراسان — سرزمین کهن

خوش آمدید Khorasaan Kabab & Gyro brings the rich, fragrant flavors of Afghanistan to Brooklyn. Named after the historic region that gave birth to poetry, trade, and legendary cuisine, our kitchen honors recipes passed down through generations.

Every kabob is marinated for hours in yogurt and Afghan spices. Every grain of basmati rice is steamed to fluffy perfection with sweet raisins, slivered almonds, and caramelized carrots — قابلی پلو, our national treasure.

From our juicy Mix Grill platter to our famous Qabeli Palow with tender lamb shank, every bite tells a story of tradition, hospitality, and love. نوش جان — bon appétit.
